What is a Healther ID?

Your Healther ID is a unique identifier that links your health profile globally, enabling seamless access to services across borders.

How to sign up?

Click on "Sign Up", fill in your details, and create your account. You'll receive a unique Healther ID immediately.

Can I use social login?

Yes! You can sign up using Google, GitHub, or Apple accounts for quick registration.
